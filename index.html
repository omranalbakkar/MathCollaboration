How it works
Up and running in minutes.
01
Admin sets up the platform
Super Admin creates grades, subjects, teacher accounts, and student accounts from the Admin Portal.
02
Teacher creates a session
Teacher logs in, creates a session for their assigned grade and subject, and builds the question bank.
03
Students answer live
Students log in securely, join the open session, and submit answers. The teacher sees progress in real time.
04
Review, grade & feedback
Teachers assign scores and written feedback. Students see their results immediately on their screen.
