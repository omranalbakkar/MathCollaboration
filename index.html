What's Inside

Built for the real classroom.

Every feature is purpose-built for live academic sessions — no filler, no fluff.

Smart Question Bank

Create open-text, multiple-choice, true/false, and numeric questions. Set points, correct answers, and subject tags — then import or export as JSON or HTML.

Live Student Monitor

Watch every student's progress in real time via a Kanban-style board. See who is absent, in progress, or completed the moment their status changes.

Instant Grading

Review each student's answers question-by-question. Assign numeric scores and written feedback that students see immediately on their screen.

Secure Role Access

Firebase Authentication with Firestore role documents. Every portal enforces backend security rules — not just hidden front-end links.

Any Device

Students join from phones, tablets, or laptops. No app install needed — just a browser and a school login to access all quizzes and results.

Firebase Sync

Powered by Firestore + Realtime Database. The free tier handles hundreds of concurrent users with sub-second sync and full offline resilience.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Four clear steps take you from a blank platform to a live graded session.

1
01

Admin sets up the platform

Super Admin creates grades, subjects, teacher accounts, and student accounts from the Admin Portal using the dashboard or bulk Excel import.

2
02

Teacher creates a session

Teacher logs in, creates a session for their assigned grade and subject, builds the question bank, and toggles the session open.

3
03

Students answer live

Students log in securely, see their available quizzes, and submit answers one-by-one. The teacher sees every response arrive in real time.

4
04

Review, grade & feedback

Teachers assign scores and written feedback per question. Students instantly see their full results and can download a detailed PDF report.
